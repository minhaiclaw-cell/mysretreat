Sandy Beach & Swimming Area

Beach & Swimming

Our pristine sandy beach features shallow entry perfect for families with young children. The crystal-clear waters of Himbury Lake are ideal for swimming, with designated swimming areas and beautiful sandy shores that stretch along our waterfront.

  • Sandy beach with shallow entry
  • Designated swimming areas
  • Beach volleyball area
  • Lakefront picnic spots
  • Sunset viewing deck
  • Beach chairs available for rent

Best Times: Early morning for peaceful swims, late afternoon for family fun

Fishing

Himbury Lake is a angler's paradise with abundant populations of bass, trout, and panfish. Whether you're an experienced angler or teaching kids to fish, our waters provide excellent opportunities year-round.

  • Bass, trout, and panfish
  • Multiple fishing spots around the lake
  • Boat launch available
  • Fishing equipment rentals
  • Cleaning station provided
  • Guided fishing tours (seasonal)

Fishing License Required: Ontario fishing license required for ages 18+

Miles of Forest Hiking Trails

Hiking Trails

Explore miles of well-maintained trails that wind through diverse ecosystems. From easy lakeside walks to more challenging forest paths, our trail system offers something for every fitness level and age group.

  • Over 10 miles of marked trails
  • Easy to challenging difficulty levels
  • Wildlife viewing opportunities
  • Scenic lookout points
  • Trail maps available
  • Bird watching stations

Wildlife Spotting: Keep an eye out for deer, beaver, various bird species, and other local wildlife

Paddling Adventures

Discover the tranquil beauty of Himbury Lake from the water. Rent a kayak or canoe to explore hidden coves, paddle the creek system, or simply enjoy a peaceful morning on the calm waters.

  • Kayak and canoe rentals
  • Creek exploration routes
  • Guided paddle tours available
  • Life jackets provided
  • Launch areas throughout property
  • Sunset paddle experiences

Rental Info: Half-day and full-day rentals available. Advance booking recommended during peak season.

Winter Activities

MysRetreat transforms into a winter wonderland when the snow falls. From ice fishing on the frozen lake to snowshoeing through quiet forests, winter offers its own special magic.

  • Snowshoeing trail network
  • Winter hiking opportunities
  • Heated cabin rentals available
  • Ice fishing equipment rentals
  • Snowshoe equipment available

Season: December through March, depending on ice and snow conditions
