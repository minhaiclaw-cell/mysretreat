Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about booking and your stay

Do you accept pets?

Yes! We're a pet-friendly campground. There's a $10/night pet fee, and pets must be leashed at all times. Please clean up after your pet and respect other guests.

Is WiFi available?

Basic WiFi is available near the main office and camp store. Coverage throughout the property is limited - perfect for a digital detox!

Can I bring my own boat?

Absolutely! We have a boat launch available for guests. All watercraft must comply with Ontario boating regulations.

Are there shower facilities?

Yes, we have clean, modern washroom and shower facilities throughout the campground. Cabins have their own private bathrooms.

Is firewood available?

Yes, seasoned firewood is available for purchase at our camp store. To protect local forests, please don't bring firewood from other areas.

What about food and supplies?

Our camp store has basic supplies, snacks, and ice. For full groceries, Kearney is 12 km away with a grocery store, LCBO, and restaurants.

Is swimming safe for children?

Our beach has shallow entry areas perfect for kids, but children should always be supervised. Life jackets are recommended and available for rent.

When is your busiest time?

July and August weekends are our busiest times. For quieter experiences, try weekdays or shoulder seasons (May-June, September).
